    Frequently Asked Questions

    Got questions before getting inked or pierced? Here's everything you need to know before stepping into the world of Valhalla Ink.

    1. Do I need to book an appointment, or do you accept walk-ins?

    We accept both! Walk-ins are welcome for smaller tattoos and piercings, but appointments are highly recommended for custom pieces and larger sessions.

    2. Is getting a tattoo or piercing painful?

    Pain levels vary depending on placement and personal tolerance. Most clients describe it as uncomfortable but bearable. Our artists and piercers work quickly and professionally to keep you as comfortable as possible.

    3. How do I prepare for my session?

    Eat a full meal, stay hydrated, and avoid alcohol or blood thinners 24 hours before your appointment. Bring a valid ID, and wear comfortable clothing that allows easy access to the area being worked on.

    4. What aftercare is required?

    We'll provide full aftercare instructions tailored to your tattoo or piercing. Generally, keep the area clean, avoid direct sunlight, and donâ€™t pick at scabs. We also offer aftercare kits in-studio.

    5. What age do I need to be to get tattooed or pierced?

    You must be at least 18 years old for tattoos. For piercings, minors (16+) may be accepted with a parent or legal guardian present, along with proper ID for both. No exceptions.
